Revolutionizing Autism Tracking with Machine Learning & Games

March 21, 2025 | by Admin | 12 Comments

At Autism Tracker, we aim to bridge technology and care to support families navigating the autism spectrum journey. Our platform integrates innovative features to enhance autism screening and engagement for children and caregivers.

1. Machine Learning for Accurate ASD Screening

We leverage the power of machine learning algorithms to analyze behavioral data from various inputs. By processing Q-Chat responses and other screening assessments, the system provides a probability score for Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) risk levels.

  • Advanced data models for early detection
  • Continuous learning with new input patterns
  • Improved decision-making for parents and therapists

2. Q-Chat Based Diagnostic Tool

The Q-Chat (Quantitative Checklist for Autism in Toddlers) is a widely recognized screening tool for ASD. Our platform allows parents and professionals to conduct this interactive questionnaire online.

  • 10 carefully designed questions
  • Real-time scoring system
  • Clear and easy-to-understand reports

3. Interactive Games for Child Engagement

We understand that children learn and express best through play. Our platform features mini-games tailored to help children improve their cognitive and emotional skills:

  • Color Recognition Game - Enhances visual processing
  • Emotion Recognition Game - Improves understanding of emotional cues
  • Sorting & Matching Games - Builds logical reasoning
  • Memory Challenge - Strengthens memory and attention

By combining technology and play, Autism Tracker provides an engaging environment for both children and their families while assisting in early ASD detection.
